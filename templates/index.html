About This Webapp

Welcome to our website where we provide accurate rainfall predictions based on data from our very own Arduino-based mini weather station. Our machine learning model analyzes temperature, humidity, and wind speed data to make predictions that are constantly updated to reflect the latest weather conditions. Our innovative approach provides more accurate and reliable predictions than traditional weather forecasting methods, allowing you to plan your activities with confidence. Start exploring our website today to discover the benefits of our unique and innovative approach to rainfall prediction.

Predictor

By analyzing real-time data from a network of weather sensors, our predictor is able to provide highly localized weather forecasts, tailored to your specific location. Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or need to know whether to bring an umbrella to work, our predictor has you covered.
